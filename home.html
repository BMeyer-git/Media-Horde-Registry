Media Horde Registry Home

Welcome!

Welcome to Media Horde Registry! This website will allow anyone who visits it to begin recording every piece of media that they own in a digital format that will last forever and allow you to easily search through the entire collection, without having to physically sift through your personal hoard. Whether it be CDs, DVDs, vinyls, casettes, books, or even videogames, the larger the collection, the more benefit you will derive from using this website. Just head over to the "Media Form" page to get started, or visit the "Instructions" page if you're having trouble.

Start a conversation

After you've submitted all of your data, you can use the "Contact Us" page to request your data, or if you notice something tha could be changed to improve the experience, or if you just want to share a story of how Media Horde Registry has helped you! Your experience matters to us, whether it was positive or negative, and contacting us is the best way to ensure it stays the same, or changes for the better. Your input will shape the future of your own collection, and Media Horde Registry itself.

Still not convinced?

If you still don't see the great help this website can be to you, we invite you to visit the "Why MHR" page, where you can learn all about why we started this website and a potential success story in the making. If your collection of media just doesn't seem large enough to have a whole spreadsheet dedicated to it, there's no time like the present! Start with what you have, and as your inventory of media grows, you'll easily be able to keep up with it, and always have an up-to-date registry of all your media at your fingertips. Get out there and start adding to the horde!
